our approach

Team 6911 uses events and programs in our community and across the world to shift people's outlooks, spark optimism about STEAM and robotics, and shape opportunities for those in Rockland County, the State of NY, and beyond.

Shift Outlooks

By inspiring the younger generation through STEAM-focused activities and engaging others through interactive learning events, SO BOTZ spreads STEAM awareness across our district, community, and the world. Our team places great emphasis on spreading STEAM outreach to the younger generation in our community.

Spark Optimism

Through several fundraisers and partnering with organizations, we strive to connect with our community. By creating fun, engaging ways to fundraise, we inspire so many to donate and support us. Our team is always looking to come up with innovative ways to spread STEAM appreciation and raise money for important causes.

Shape Opportunities

We offer several opportunities through our district and in our community. We create lessons, host meetings, mentor and assist teams and clubs. We give people of various ages and from all over the world the chance to explore STEAM in a variety of ways.
